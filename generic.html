Social Media Platform for MealSavvy

An app that provides affordable meal plans for UC Berkeley students

During CalHacks 5.0s, our team BulletPoint worked with another team DalaodrinkJava to build a Social Media app including UI Design, Development and Testing. This will be integrated as the Social Media Platform for a startup MealSavvy, which provides affordable meal plans for UC Berkeley students at restaurants around Berkeley. We used MealSavvy's APIs to send HTTP requests to the servers to pull users' information in JSON format and built social networks for them. Users can see other their friends' transaction details and like their status and give comments, which helps to build a user-friendly and interactive community within the app. We used Xcode and Swift to write the majority of the code and realize the full functionality and the good appearance of the app. Here is the link to our submission to CalHacks 5.0: Social Media Platform for MealSavvy on HackerEarth and the Youtube demo link: Social Media platform for MealSavvy Demo
